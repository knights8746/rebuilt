WHERE IS YOUR MONEY GOING?

Why fundraise?

As a sponsor, you will play a vital role in helping our team achieve its goals for the upcoming FRC season. Your support will have a significant impact on several aspects of our program.

Technical Innovation

By contributing to our team, your help wil allow us to access materials and technology that will ensure that our entry for the FRC competition will be a robot that can compete at the highest level.

STEM Education

As a sponsor, you will also support the spread of STEM education among the younger community. Donating will help us organize summer camps, events, and presentations.

Professional Development

With your contribution, students will be gaining important life skills in leadership, organization, and time management. Your help supports young leaders with skills that will help them in their career paths.

Competition Success

Your donation helps us attend regional and national competitions, where we can display your commitment and our dedication to FIRST Roboics ideals.

As our sponsor, We would like to recognize your contribution appropriately. If you choose to become a sponsor, we are more than happy to prominently display your company name and logo on our robot, team shirts, and promotional materials. Additionally, we offer personalized sponsorship benefits, tailored to your company. We are open to discussing a package that aligns with your business's objectives and expectations.
